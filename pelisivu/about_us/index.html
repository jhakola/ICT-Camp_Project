This website was made as a learning project for the two-month-long ICT Camp course, the aim of which is to help you find your path into the field of ICT. For our final project, our five-person team focused on learning how to create and host a website, in this case a gaming site with a retro feel to it. The example game created for the site is a simple version of the thought experiment Prisoner's Dilemma, implementing five different strategies to choose from. The goal of our project was to learn not only about coding, hosting, testing, and so forth, but also about the principles of agile projects and teamwork.
